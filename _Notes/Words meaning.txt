## Words Meaning
- `PCB` — Printed circuit board
- `SMD`	— surface-mount device
- `SMT`	— surface-mount technology
- `Thru-Hole` — Through-hole technology (also spelled "thru-hole") THT
- `LED`	— Light Emitting Diode
- `SOT-23 Package` — Small-outline transistor
- `TSOT-23 package` — thin small outline transistor
- `W2B Connectors` — Wire-to-Board Connectors
- `SOP`	— Small Outline Package
- `EMI`	— Electromagnetic Interference
- `Pitch` — The "pitch" is the center-to-center spacing of pins or holes
